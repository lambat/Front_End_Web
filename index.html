About Us

Welcome to Millennium DIY, the online retailer to the building trade and the DIY enthusiasts. Millennium DIY is a family business founded in 1987 and is located in the heart of Leicester. We decided to launch online sales to keep up to date with the current retail demands and offer a wide range of high quality products to a larger audience. By offering quality products at discounted prices, we aim to further establish a stronghold in the retail sector. Combine this with our vastly experience sales and technical team, we believe we can offer you the range of services to meet your needs.

If you have any questions about our products or have any technical issues, please do not hesitate to contact us.
