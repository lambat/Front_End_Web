About Us

Millennium DIY is a small family business and was established in 1987. It is situated in the heart of the city and serves both the building trade and the DIY enthusiasts. The business has grown from strength to strength focusing on excellent customer service and competitive pricing. The business is known for proving expert advice on all DIY matters.
